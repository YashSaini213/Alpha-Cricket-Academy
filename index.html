About Us

Shaping Future Champions

Discover our vision, mission, and the team behind Alpha Cricket Academy

Our Vision

To be the premier cricket coaching academy in the region, nurturing talent and instilling a love for the game in players of all ages and skill levels.

Our Mission

To provide world-class cricket training with a focus on technical excellence, physical fitness, and mental toughness. We believe in developing not just skilled cricketers, but well-rounded individuals who carry the values of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship beyond the pitch.
